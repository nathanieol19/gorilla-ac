When should you call a professional?


Check your filter

Make sure to check your air conditioning filter and that you are changing them every six months. WHen filters become clogged than systems are no longer able to deliver the proper amount of air flow and they will start to freeze the indoor coils.


Check your circuit Breaker

Circuit Breakers may flip off on hotter days due to the strain put on compressors.


Check your thermostat batteries

Batteries on thermostat may cause system to not respond. Make sure you are changing them at least once a year.
